Tesla

Wat zegt Tesla zelf

"All new Tesla cars have the hardware needed in the future for full self-driving in almost all circumstances. The system is designed to be able to conduct short and long distance trips with no action required by the person in the driver's seat. All you will need to do is get in and tell your car where to go. If you don't say anything, the car will look at your calendar and take you there as the assumed destination or just home if nothing is on the calendar. Your Tesla will figure out the optimal route, navigate urban streets (even without lane markings), manage complex intersections with traffic lights, stop signs and roundabouts, and handle densely packed freeways with cars moving at high speed. When you arrive at your destination, simply step out at the entrance and your car will enter park seek mode, automatically search for a spot and park itself. A tap on your phone summons it back to you. The future use of these features without supervision is dependent on achieving reliability far in excess of human drivers as demonstrated by billions of miles of experience, as well as regulatory approval, which may take longer in some jurisdictions. As these self-driving capabilities are introduced, your car will be continuously upgraded through over-the-air software updates."

Tesla heeft het heel slim aangepakt, wat betreft de zelfrijdende auto's. Wat je namelijk is het stukje hierboven leest, is dat Tesla hun nieuwere auto's heeft uitgerust met de hardware die nodig is om de auto zelf te laten rijden. Hierdoor hoeven ze alleen nog de software te updaten als die klaar is. Dan kan elke Tesla bestuurder de software downloaden en daarmee de auto zelf laten rijden. Tesla wilde nooit meteen een autonoom systeem, maar wilde langzamerhand meer functies toe voegen die de auto's steeds ietsje meer autonoom maakte. De auto's van Tesla beschikken over zeer goede camera's die 360 graden om de auto heen kunnen 'zien'. Dit wordt doorgegeven aan een van de beste boardcomputers op de markt, die alles op zijn beurt weer doorgeeft aan de auto.
